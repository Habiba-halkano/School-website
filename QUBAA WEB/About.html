BRIEF HISTORY OF QUBAA MUSLIM SCHOOL (from 1491-1987) The school started in the premises of Qubaa mosque in majengo near MAJENGO sokoni in Mombasa after which the school was named QUBAA PRIMARY SCHOOL. In January, 14th 1987 recruitment of pupils started and the school opened its doors as a government aided school having established four classes namely B1, G1,B2 and G2. The founders of the school were; STAFF MEMBERS Mr. Awadh Mbarak-Head Teacher Mr. Kombo Mohammed Daud-Teacher Mr. Maamun Abubakar-Teacher Mrs. Zulekha Ali-Teacher Mr. Mohamed Bashrahil-Teacher BOARD MEMBERS Mr. M.S. Bawazir Mr. Said Hamed Said Mr. Rashid Sajad In January 1999 Qubaa Primary School changed to a private school and was named Qubaa Academy and later changes to QUBAA MUSLIM SCHOOL MISSION To bring up a generation which embraces both Islamic knowledge and life styles, academically competent individuals who are well equipped with modern technology and are a reflection of morality to the society. VISSION To offer quality education for the future generations involving the mind and the soul since conscience is illuminated by both science and religion, and wisdom occurs from the combination of the two.
